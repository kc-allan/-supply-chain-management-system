How It Works

Learn how {{ app_name }} ensures the authenticity and quality of fertilizers from manufacturer to farmer using blockchain technology.

Step 1: Manufacturing

The manufacturer records the production of a batch of fertilizer on the blockchain, including details such as the composition, production date, and batch number. A digital certificate of authenticity is assigned to ensure it meets regulatory standards.

Step 2: Distribution to Wholesaler

When the manufacturer ships the batch to a wholesaler, a new transaction is recorded on the blockchain, linking the batch to the wholesaler. The wholesaler can verify the batch's authenticity and details through the blockchain.

Step 3: Transfer to Retailer

The wholesaler records the transfer of the batch to a retailer on the blockchain. The retailer inspects the batch and validates its authenticity using the blockchain, ensuring that no fake fertilizer is added midway.

Step 4: Sale to Farmer

When the retailer sells the fertilizer to a farmer, the final transaction is recorded on the blockchain. The farmer can verify the entire history of the fertilizer batch, ensuring its authenticity and quality.
